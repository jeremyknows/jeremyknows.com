I was an actor, then a clown, improviser, and puppeteer. Then a teacher and director. When Covid shut everything down in 2020, I pivoted, photography, handyman work to pay the bills in NYC.

In 2021, I found blockchain, crypto, and NFTs. Through rapid public experimentation and a refusal to quit, I built a following and landed my dream job. Different context, same skills.
